# Privacy Policy for TrackMySubs

**Effective Date:** June 26, 2026

TrackMySubs ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the TrackMySubs mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Local Storage Only
TrackMySubs is designed as a local-first utility. 
* **Subscription Data:** All information you enter about your subscriptions—such as subscription names, categories, pricing, billing cycles, notes, and renewal dates—is stored strictly on your device using a local Room database and Android DataStore.
* **No Server Uploads:** We do not host servers to collect, upload, or transmit your subscription details. Your data remains entirely under your control on your physical device.

### Personal Data
We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the core features of the App.

---

## 2. Device Permissions

To provide standard functionality, the App may request the following permissions:
* **Notifications:** Used to schedule and deliver local alerts reminding you of upcoming subscription renewals or free trial expirations (e.g., 7 days, 3 days, or 1 day prior). These alerts are generated locally on your device and do not involve external messaging services.
* **Internet access (if applicable):** Used exclusively for processing premium upgrades via Google Play Billing.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates Google Play Services and Google Play Billing to process premium subscription upgrades. These third-party services may collect information used to identify you in accordance with their respective privacy policies:
* [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We do not integrate any external analytics, trackers, or advertising SDKs.

---

## 4. Data Security

We care about safeguarding your data. Because your data is stored locally:
* Security of your information depends on the security of your device. We recommend securing your device with a PIN, pattern, or biometric lock.
* Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored subscription records unless backed up by standard Android system-level backups (which are managed by your Google account).

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document and, if appropriate, providing an in-app notice.
